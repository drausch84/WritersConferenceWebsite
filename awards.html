Author Story Title Story Summary

Johnathan Crane

The Beaver Pelts A terrifying short story about the price a father will pay to shape his son in

Rachel Mewberg

Where My Heart Drifted Slowly A story that follows the everyday struggle of a single mother of four children who
begins to wax nostalgic over prior dreams of becoming an actress.

Anika Laghari

The Withering Leaves A farmer outside Lachen in India discovers an ivory ox which his family
believes is an omen of things to come.

Justin Dickerson

Broken Reflections After returning from a deployment in Iraq, a young man comes to terms
with who he's become and who he might still become.
