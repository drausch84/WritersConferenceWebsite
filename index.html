It's that time of year again! It's time for the annual Denver Writer's Conference held at The Colorado Convention Center. The Denver Writer's Conference is a perfect chance for established and aspiring writers of all genres to hone their skills and learn from some of the best and brightest in the industry. Choose from a variety of fully-catered workshops presented by industry professionals and renowned author presenters.

Attendee Information

All attendees will have the opportunity to pitch ideas to agents, participate in critique sessions,  and network with their peers. Some of the agents/editors participating in the conference include: Ryan Shasman,  Greg Yanis, Stefanie Holden. Rebecca Housman, and Lucy Stiles. For a full list of participants, email us at Denver Writer's Conference.

We ask all attendees to refrain from the use of any recording equipment during any of the sessions or workshops.  Failure to comply may result in expulsion from the conference without a refund.
